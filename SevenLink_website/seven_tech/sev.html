Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you complete my document formatting?

Most Word documents (under 50 pages) are completed within 24 hours. For urgent requests (3-6 hour turnaround), we charge a 30% premium fee.

Can I get my bound thesis delivered same day at UENR?

Yes! Our UENR binding service offers same-day completion if ordered before 2pm. Delivery to your department adds just ₵5-₵10 depending on location.

Do you help with coding assignments last-minute?

We prioritize urgent coding help, but recommend contacting us at least 48 hours before deadline. Last-minute requests (under 12 hours) incur a 50% rush fee.

How do I pay for services?

We accept Mobile Money (all networks), cash (UENR only), and bank transfers. Payment is 50% upfront for new clients, balance upon completion.

Can you do group discounts for classmates?

Absolutely! Groups of 3+ students get 15% off document services, and 20% off coding projects when ordered together.
